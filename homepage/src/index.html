Tachi

Tachi is a powerful, modular and modern rhythm game score tracker.

Track Your Scores.

Tachi supports a bunch of your favourite games, and integrates with many existing services to make sure no score is lost to the void. Furthermore, it's backed by an Open-Source API, so your scores are always available!

Analyse Your Scores.

Tachi analyses your scores for you, breaking them down into all the statistics you'll ever need. No more spreadsheets!

Provide Cool Features.

Tachi implements the features rhythm gamers already talk about. Break your scores down into sessions, set goals, showcase your best metrics on your profile, study your progress on folders - it's all there, and done for you!
